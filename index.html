When Fireworks Go Wrong

By Hailey Anderton

Americans love their fireworks. Whether it's a national holiday or simply the friendly neighborhood pyromaniac's birthday, it's a safe bet there will be fireworks present. During the summer months, especially around Independence Day, there are a significant amount of recorded fireworks-related injuries. The charts below illustrate a breakdown of all fireworks-related injuries that took place between June 20-July 20, 2014. The column chart breaks down these occurances by age group and gender. The pie chart displays which bodily regions took the hardest beating during the given time period.
